# Transfer Orders

Transfer Orders in HotWax OMS are used to move inventory 
between facilities such as warehouses and stores.

## What You'll Find Here

- **Overview** — What Transfer Orders are and how they work
- **Statuses & Lifecycle** — All possible statuses explained

## Key Concepts

Transfer Orders help track inventory movement between 
facilities. They ensure stock is accounted for at every 
stage of the transfer process.
