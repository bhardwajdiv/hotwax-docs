# Overview

Transfer Orders are documents used in HotWax OMS to 
manage the movement of inventory between facilities.

## What is a Transfer Order?

A Transfer Order records the intent to move stock from 
one location (source) to another (destination).

## Types of Transfers

### Warehouse to Store
Moving inventory from a central warehouse to a 
retail store location.

### Store to Store
Moving inventory between two retail store locations 
to balance stock levels.

### Store to Warehouse
Returning inventory from a store back to the 
central warehouse.

## Why Transfer Orders Matter

- Ensures accurate inventory counts at all facilities
- Provides a clear audit trail of stock movement
- Helps prevent stockouts at high-demand locations
